Explaination of terms

  • Bitcoin Trade Volume: this is indicate how many Bitcoin are being bought and sold on specific exchange. For instance, when people deposit and withdraw funds. It's also a good indicator of the general interest in the crypto market

  • Bitcoin Transaction Volume Ratio: is a value metric that helps investor understand if the Bitcoin's market value is high or low, which help them to buy low and sell high.

  • Bitcoin Miner Revenue: this is the value that indicate how much does a Bitcoin miner make within a day of mining Bitcoin.
